PhotoBackup

Backup your pictures from your mobile device to your server.
OWN YOUR DATA!

PhotoBackup works with two applications, a client and a server, dedicated to the backup of the pictures you take on your mobile device to your own server.

You need four little steps:

  1. Install a mobile application ;
  2. Install one of the server applications ;
  3. Take pictures as usual and let the magic happens in background ;
  4. Keep your pictures safe on your server.
